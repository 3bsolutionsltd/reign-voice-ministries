The Story Behind Our Building

For 12 years, our congregation has faithfully worked towards constructing a permanent home for worship. What began as a vision has grown through the dedication of our community. We've completed the ring beam stage and are now moving to roofing - bringing us closer to completion!

As we approach this crucial roofing phase, we trust in God's provision through partners like you who believe in this mission. Every contribution brings us closer to completing a space where generations will gather to worship, learn, and grow in faith.

Our Pastor

Welcome to Reign Voice of God Ministries International! We are a church family devoted to worshiping God, growing in faith, and serving our community with love.

For 12 years, we have been building not just a physical structure, but a spiritual home where people encounter God's presence and experience His transforming power. Our vision is to see Soronko Village and beyond impacted by the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Whether you're visiting for the first time or have been part of our family for years, we're grateful you're here. Come join us as we build God's kingdom together!
